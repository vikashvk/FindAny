Services And Modules
That Complete Our System

ADMIN

Add New Product

The main aim of this module is to add new product to catalogue. Admin can add information about a product such as its name, category and price for the Products page in the admin.

  • From our FindAny home, go to Admin -> Login
  • After Admin Login page, click Add Product
  • Enter the Product Name, its category and its price.
  • Click Add

ADMIN

View All Product

The main aim of this module is to update and delete existing products from catalogue. Admin can edit information about a product such as its name, category and price for that Product. Admin can delete that product as well.

  • From our FindAny home, go to Admin -> Login
  • After Admin Login page, click View Products
  • View the Product Name, its category and its price. You can update or delete that product.
  • Take Action

CUSTOMER

Shopping Time

The main aim of this module is that customer can add new product to catalogue. Customer can shop and add products to their cart.


  • From our FindAny home, go to Customer -> Login
  • After Customer Login page, browse Products
  • Add the Products
  • Click Cart
